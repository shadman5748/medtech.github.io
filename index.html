About Us

Best Medical Care For Yourself and Your Family

MedTech is a web-based application that helps manage the information related to health care and aids in the job completion of health care providers and users effectively. They manage the data related to all the departments, reputed and certified doctors with their full academic and medical info, appointing time, creating appointments for the patients, their health info, health conditions, admission info, billing info and receipt, contact info of registered doctor etc. Currently in this pandemic situation, we are not safe to go outside even for the medical healthcare support. So, we need a platform, from which we can access to a healthcare diagnostics center or can have access to all the registered and certified doctors in a single page. Doctors can also keep in contact with his patients and have the access to contact with them directly as well as can follow the track record of the patient’s health conditions and can give advice according to their needs. By which he doesn’t have to go the hospital to check up the patients.
